# Gig Economy Benefits Pool Smart Contracts\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces a comprehensive blockchain-based benefits system for gig workers, implemented as three interconnected Clarity smart contracts on the Stacks blockchain. The system provides automated earnings tracking, contribution calculation, and benefit claim processing for gig economy participants.\n\n## Smart Contract Architecture\n\n### 1. Earnings Tracking Oracle (`earnings-tracking-oracle.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Integration with gig platforms to track worker earnings and work hours across multiple platforms.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- Multi-platform earnings aggregation\n- Real-time work hour verification\n- Historical earnings data storage\n- Platform verification and registration system\n- Worker profile management with fraud prevention\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-platform`: Allows contract owner to register verified gig platforms\n- `register-worker`: Enables gig workers to join the benefits pool\n- `record-earnings`: Records earnings and work hours from integrated platforms\n- `get-worker-earnings`: Retrieves comprehensive worker earnings data\n- `get-worker-average-rate`: Calculates average hourly rates across platforms\n\n**Lines of Code**: 277 lines\n\n### 2. Benefits Contribution Calculator (`benefits-contribution-calculator.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Automated calculation of health insurance and retirement contributions based on earnings tiers.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- Tiered contribution system based on earnings levels\n- Automated health insurance and retirement fund allocation\n- Monthly contribution tracking and payment verification\n- Annual projection calculations\n- Dynamic rate adjustment mechanisms\n\n**Contribution Tiers**:\n- **Basic Tier** (≤5,000 STX): 6% health, 4% retirement\n- **Standard Tier** (5,001-15,000 STX): 8% health, 6% retirement  \n- **Premium Tier** (>15,000 STX): 10% health, 8% retirement\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `enroll-worker`: Enrolls workers in the benefits program\n- `calculate-contributions`: Computes contributions based on earnings and tier\n- `process-monthly-contribution`: Handles monthly contribution processing\n- `get-worker-profile`: Retrieves worker contribution history\n- `calculate-annual-projection`: Projects annual contribution requirements\n\n**Lines of Code**: 378 lines\n\n### 3. Claim Verification System (`claim-verification-system.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Processes and verifies benefit claims with automated payout mechanisms and fraud detection.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- Multi-type claim processing (medical, unemployment, disability, emergency)\n- Automated fraud detection and prevention\n- Instant payout processing for verified claims\n- Comprehensive claim lifecycle management\n- Authorized verifier system with performance tracking\n\n**Claim Types & Limits**:\n- **Medical Claims**: Up to 5,000 STX\n- **Unemployment Claims**: Up to 3,000 STX\n- **Disability Claims**: Up to 8,000 STX\n- **Emergency Claims**: Up to 2,000 STX\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `submit-claim`: Allows workers to submit benefit claims\n- `verify-claim`: Enables authorized verifiers to approve/reject claims\n- `process-payout`: Handles automatic payouts for approved claims\n- `get-claim`: Retrieves claim details and status\n- `authorize-verifier`: Admin function to authorize claim verifiers\n\n**Lines of Code**: 471 lines\n\n## Technical Implementation Details\n\n### Security Features\n- **Access Control**: Contract owner restrictions for administrative functions\n- **Fraud
